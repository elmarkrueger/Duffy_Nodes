A hyper-realistic yet dreamlike anthropomorphic black duck stands defiantly in the center of a warping metropolitan boulevard in a profound Surrealist masterpiece. His obsidian feathers possess a wet, iridescent sheen, catching fragments of a shattered, kaleidoscopic sky. His large, expressive orange beak appears to be slowly liquefying at the edges, dripping golden nectar onto a street that has transformed into a polished mirror reflecting a distorted, impossible reality. The duck’s eyes are deep cosmic wells containing swirling nebulae and distant stars. Surrounding him, the architecture of the city twists like pulled taffy, with streetlamps bending gracefully toward him as if whispering secrets. The background figures are indistinct, ethereal silhouettes composed of golden dust and floating clockwork gears. The composition is a dramatic low-angle shot that exaggerates the duck's presence against the melting horizon and curving skyscrapers. Lighting is a complex interplay of ethereal volumetric moonbeams and a harsh, subterranean amber glow rising from the cracks in the pavement, creating high-contrast shadows. The overall medium is a masterful blend of surrealist oil painting and high-fidelity digital art, featuring rich textures, subsurface scattering on the beak, and impossible geometries.

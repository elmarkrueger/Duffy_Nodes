A minimalist vector illustration of a sleek black duck character standing confidently in a centered composition. The duck features a smooth, solid matte-black body with sharp, clean contours and absolutely no feather texture. It has a large, vibrant flat-orange beak and matching oversized orange webbed feet that ground the character. Its eyes are simple, expressive white ovals with black pupils, capturing a classic animation essence through a modern lens. The scene is stripped of all complexity, set against a flat, monochromatic off-white background with a single, subtle horizontal line to suggest a clean, empty street. The lighting is uniform and flat, completely avoiding gradients, soft shadows, or complex highlights to maintain a pure graphic design aesthetic. The style is heavily inspired by Bauhaus principles and contemporary minimalist art, focusing on geometric simplicity and high-contrast color blocking. Every line is deliberate and refined, resulting in a bold, iconic silhouette that stands out against the void of the background. There are no background characters or urban clutter, focusing entirely on the essential form of the subject.
